Hi, I'm Poornima – the human behind The Hangry Scientist. I grew up in Bangalore and now call San Francisco home. 

I'm a scientist by training and I cook the same way. Tweak, taste, adjust, and document until I get it right. This blog is my recipe notebook – a place to capture the versions I'm finally happy with, share it easily with friends and family, and keep everything in one spot. 

Side note to family & friends – if you have a recipe you'd like me to document, send it my way! You'll get full credit and I get to taste test! 😄
